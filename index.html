Who I Am

Building networks.
Engineering clarity.

A brief look at the professional behind the sites, dashboards, and automation pipelines.

I'm a DEC Engineer at Nokia with over three years of hands-on experience delivering critical LTE and 5G infrastructure to some of the world's leading telecom operators — T-Mobile, Verizon, Bharti Airtel, TPG, and Spark.

My work sits at the intersection of network delivery and data engineering. I don't just validate sites — I build the tools that give project teams real-time visibility into what's been done, what's at risk, and what's next. That means Power BI dashboards, SQL pipelines, Power Automate workflows, and Grafana-based compliance reporting.

I was recognised with the Business Excellence Award 2025 for digitizing Nokia's Health & Safety validation process — cutting audit preparation time by 60% and replacing paper-based forms with a fully digital workflow.

I'm a Nokia Level 2 Certified Data Engineer. My technical philosophy is simple: if you're spending time on something repetitive, automate it. If you can't measure it, instrument it.
